An AI-powered resume analyzer that evaluates how well your resume matches a job description, simulating how Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) parse and score resumes. Helps job seekers optimize their resumes before submission.
Working:
1. Paste the job description
2. Upload your resume
3. Get instant analysis including:
   - Match score (0-100%)
   - Missing keywords
   - Professional summary
   - Custom recommendations
